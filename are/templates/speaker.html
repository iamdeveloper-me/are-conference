About speakers

The ARE Conference aims to bring the global leaders on one platform to deliberate on the climate crisis and to pledge concrete actions on mitigating it.
We are thankful for the following Eminent Speakers to grace the conference with their presence.

Dr. Anil Kakodkar

Padma Vibhushan Awardee

Dr. Anil Kakodkar was the Director of BARC and the Chairman of the Atomic Energy Commission, and Secretary to the Government of India, Department of Atomic Energy. He was amongst the chosen few involved in the first successful Peaceful Nuclear Explosion Experiment that India conducted on May 18, 1974, at Pokhran. He was awarded the Padma Vibhushan, India's second-highest civilian honor, on 26 January 2009.

Ms. Gauri Singh

Deputy Director-General, IRENA

Gauri Singh is the Deputy Director-General of the International Renewable Energy Agency. Ms. Singh brings over 30 years of experience in policy, advocacy, and project implementation within the field of renewable energy and sustainable development from India and the international system. She was responsible for leading the development of the National Solar Mission of India policy in 2010, an early policy framework designed to drive solar power development across the country.

Ms. Dia Mirza

Actor, Producer, UN Environment Goodwill Ambassador & United Nations Secretary-General Advocate for Sustainable Development

Dia Mirza is an award-winning Actor, Producer. Today, Dia Mirza has become the voice of environmental and wildlife conservation in the country and a torchbearer for causes related to nature. She was appointed as the United Nations Environment Goodwill Ambassador for India at the UNEA assembly held in Nairobi.

Prof. Anil Sahasrabudhe

Chairman, All India Council for Technical Education

Prof. Anil Sahasrabudhe, Professor of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Guwahati joined the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) as Chairman on 17th July 2015. He has been bestowed with several awards which include "; the Maha-Intrapreneur Award-2011" of Praj Industries, Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar (Life Time Achievement Award) from MIT World Peace University, Pune in 2019, Mahatma Gandhi Leadership award from Indian Achievers Forum and CSR Times (2019). He has also been conferred Ravi J Matthai National Fellow Award by AIMS (2021).

Dr. Anjal Prakash

Prof. N.H. Ravindranath

Professor (Retd.), Indian Institute of Science Bangalore, India

Prof. Ravindranath has focused his research, advocacy and publications on various dimensions of Climate Change -Mitigation Assessment, Greenhouse Gas Emissions, Impact of Climate Change and Vulnerability Assessment in Forest and Agro-ecosystems. He is an author of several IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change) Assessment Reports on Climate Change – WMO and UNEP focusing on mitigation in the forest sector.

Dr. Divya Sharma

Executive Director of Climate Group

Divya Sharma is the India Executive Director of Climate Group. In this capacity, she leads a team of professionals who work with businesses and governments around critical systems on climate action, ranging from industries, transport, energy, and the built environment. Divya is an international climate and urban development expert, specializing in climate resilience and urban development planning with almost 20 years in India and internationally.

Ms. Shloka Nath

Chief Executive Officer, India Climate Collaborative; Head - Sustainability and Policy and Advocacy, Tata Trusts

Shloka Nath currently leads the Policy and Advocacy and Sustainability and Special Projects portfolio at the Tata Trusts. She is also the Acting Director of the India Climate Collaborative, an India-led platform founded in 2018 by a group of philanthropies interested in continuing to accelerate India’s development, while also exceeding its climate goals.

Mr. Anil Bellary

Anil has more than 33 years of experience working in program management, project development, matchmaking, policy analysis, training and capacity building, and outreach and knowledge management in the areas of energy efficiency, renewable energy, climate change and environment. Currently, Anil is the Co-Director in the GIZ-KFW Project –Indo-German Energy Forum (IGEF) Support Office based in New Delhi.

Dr. Harish Hande

Founder and Chairman, SELCO India

Dr. Harish Hande is the co-founder and Chairman of SELCO Solar Light Pvt Ltd, that provides affordable renewable energy services to the poor and underserved households in rural India. He and his company have received many national and international awards, including the Ashden Award (2005 and 2007), The Tech Museum award (2005), Khemka Social Entrepreneur of the Year (2007) and the Financial Times–ArcelorMittal Boldness in Business Award (2009). He was awarded with Asia's prestigious Ramon Magsaysay Award for 2011 for “his pragmatic efforts to put solar power technology in the hands of the poor, through his social enterprise SELCO India”

Mr. Aviram Rozin

Founder and International Director of Sadhana Forest

Aviram Rozin is the Founder and International Director of Sadhana Forest, an international, volunteer-based organization focused on creating long-term food security through environmental restoration. Sadhana Forest won third prize at The Humanitarian Water and Food Awards in Denmark, in 2010. Mr. Rozin is a member of the Global Restoration Council. He and his family live in the experimental township of Auroville, South India, where their dedication to ecological restoration and their low-carbon footprint lifestyle has inspired many.

Mr. Binu Jacob

Executive Director at Greenpeace India

Mr. Binu Jacob, Executive Director, Greenpeace India has an enriching experience in the field of climate change. He at multiple occasions called for tough decisions on the part of India to address climate change. He has emphasized for India to take tough decisions to address climate issues at the same time, it is the need for the developed countries to step up and take more responsibilities that help developing countries in climate adaptation and mitigation.

Mr. Shikhar Jain

He represents CII in various committees of the Bureau of Indian Standards, National Safety Council, GRI-G4/Standards, Accountability, UK. Shri Gopal Arya

He has been entrusted with the responsibility of the National Convenor of the RSS Paryavaran Vibhag, an all-India group dedicated to improving the environment.
