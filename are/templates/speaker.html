About speakers

The ARE Conference aims to bring the global leaders on one platform to deliberate on the climate crisis and to pledge concrete actions on mitigating it.
We are thankful for the following Eminent Speakers to grace the conference with their presence.

Dr. Anil Kakodkar

Padma Vibhushan Awardee

Dr. Anil Kakodkar was the Director of BARC and the Chairman of the Atomic Energy Commission, and Secretary to the Government of India, Department of Atomic Energy. He was amongst the chosen few involved in the first successful Peaceful Nuclear Explosion Experiment that India conducted on May 18, 1974, at Pokhran. He was awarded the Padma Vibhushan, India's second-highest civilian honor, on 26 January 2009.

Ms. Gauri Singh

Deputy Director-General, IRENA

Gauri Singh is the Deputy Director-General of the International Renewable Energy Agency. Ms. Singh brings over 30 years of experience in policy, advocacy, and project implementation within the field of renewable energy and sustainable development from India and the international system. She was responsible for leading the development of the National Solar Mission of India policy in 2010, an early policy framework designed to drive solar power development across the country.

Ms. Dia Mirza

Actor, Producer, UN Environment Goodwill Ambassador & United Nations Secretary-General Advocate for Sustainable Development

Dia Mirza is an award-winning Actor, Producer. Today, Dia Mirza has become the voice of environmental and wildlife conservation in the country and a torchbearer for causes related to nature. She was appointed as the United Nations Environment Goodwill Ambassador for India at the UNEA assembly held in Nairobi.

Prof. Anil Sahasrabudhe

Chairman, All India Council for Technical Education

Prof. Anil Sahasrabudhe, Professor of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Guwahati joined the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) as Chairman on 17th July 2015. He has been bestowed with several awards which include "; the Maha-Intrapreneur Award-2011" of Praj Industries, Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar (Life Time Achievement Award) from MIT World Peace University, Pune in 2019, Mahatma Gandhi Leadership award from Indian Achievers Forum and CSR Times (2019). He has also been conferred Ravi J Matthai National Fellow Award by AIMS (2021).
