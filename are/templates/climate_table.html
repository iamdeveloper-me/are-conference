Climate Hackathon Challenge No: 2

Title

What industries should do to mitigate climate change?

Who can participate?

Anyone can participate.

Description

Climate is not changing, it has changed. It is already having an impact on the lives of millions of people across the world. Climate change has been identified as one of the biggest global threats. Every individual is using energy and material and hence contributing to climate change. Industries consume a significant amount of energy and resources and contribute to climate change. You are required to present your idea in writing about what action needs to be taken by the industries to mitigate climate change. Your solution can be just a workable idea, a suggestion for technology, an alternative way of doing things or any other innovative idea that can help industries to reduce its impact on climate change.

What participants need to submit?

Participants must submit their write-ups via a web form.

How will the evaluation be done?

The evaluation will be carried out by the research scientists who are working at grassroots level for climate action.

What participants will get?

  • There are total of 5 awards. Top 5 winners will receive Rs 11,000 each.

  • Certificates of rewards for winners.

  • The best idea is selected to speak at the conference, and two other ideas are selected to present a poster at the conference.

  • In addition, the Jury may select some of the best ideas for a "Certificate of Appreciation".

Guidelines

  • The structure of the content should be: Title, Address the problem (Maximum 300 words), Provide a solution to the problem you're addressing (Maximum 300 words), Elaborate on the solution to the problem you mentioned above (Maximum 500 words), Conclusion

  • Font size and writing style should be 12 and Times New Roman, respectively.

  • Lines should be 1.5 inches apart.

  • Participants may submit diagrams, figures, and tabular data with elaborative descriptions.

  • Participants must submit their ideas in English only.

  • The entries shall be submitted in word or PDF format. (No Scan)

    • For queries, kindly contact on conference@energyswaraj.org

Climate Hackathon Challenge No: 8

Title

Paint a T-shirt with message related to climate change and its solutions

Who can participate?

Anyone from any discipline is welcome to participate.

Description

Climate is not changing, it has changed. It is already affecting the lives of millions of people across the world. Climate change has been identified as one of the biggest global threats. Every individual is using energy and material and hence contributing to climate change. The theme of the t-shirt painting is "Climate Change and Its solution". How climate change is adversely affecting humans, human life and planet and what actions need to be taken? This is your best chance to showcase your skills on a global scale. Paint something jaw dropping to generate curiosity among masses on climate change so that they think about taking some action immediately.

What participants need to submit?

Participants need to send their hand-painted t shirts through post to the address below. Energy Swaraj Foundation, Room No. 6, Second Floor, New Hetal Apartment CHS, Near Deepali Travels, N S Road, Mulund West, Mumbai 400080.

How will the evaluation be done?

Submissions will be judged on the basis of creativity, concept, visual impact, color scheme and effective conveying of the theme.

What participants will get?

  • There are a total of 5 awards. Top 5 winners will receive Rs 11,000 each.

  • Certificates of rewards for winners.

  • The selected t-shirts will be displayed at the conference and worn by Prof. Chetan Singh Solanki during his Energy Swaraj Yatra.

  • In addition, the Jury may select some of the best ideas for a "Certificate of Appreciation".

Guidelines

  • Only plain white t-shirts in sizes "L" or "40" are allowed.

  • Participants must use only fabric or acrylic paints. It is strictly forbidden to use any other materials or ready-made stickers.

  • Hand painting only.
